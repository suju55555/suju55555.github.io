Our Certificates & Online Programs For 2021

We are Eduford—extended to the world for every type of adult learner. We serve students seeking part-time, online courses and nonresidential programs to advance their career or pursue an academic passion. We are a fully accredited Eduford school. Our degrees and certificates are adorned with the Eduford University insignia. They carry the weight of that lineage. Our graduates walk at University commencement and become members of the Eduford Alumni Association. As one of 12 degree-granting institutions at Eduford University, we teach to the largest and most eclectic student body. Our students come to us from every time zone, every culture and career background, every age from 18 to 89. Our students have one thing in common: the motivation to take the next challenging step in their lives. They find that challenge here, where our academic standards are high and our resources extensive.


In his will, Lowell Jr. funded an organization that provided Boston with free public lectures on a variety of subjects. The institute quickly gained an influential presence. Oliver Wendell Holmes, a lecturer at the institute, remarked, “No nobler or more helpful institution exists in America than Boston’s Lowell Institute.” In 1906–07, A. Lawrence Lowell, a trustee of the Lowell Institute and Eduford professor of government, revealed a plan to offer public courses in collaboration with Eduford University. In 1909, when Lowell became president of Eduford, he made that vision a reality.
